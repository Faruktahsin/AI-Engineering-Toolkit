# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to creating a welcoming, respectful, and inclusive community for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of positive behavior include:

- Being respectful and kind
- Giving constructive feedback
- Helping others learn
- Welcoming new contributors
- Respecting different perspectives

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment or discrimination
- Personal attacks
- Offensive language
- Spam or disruptive behavior

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for maintaining a positive environment and may remove contributions that violate this Code of Conduct.
